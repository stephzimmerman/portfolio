Lyrebird

2024
Website

Lyrebird is a website that allows visitors to trigger audio loops of me vocally recreating various sounds (wind, traffic, birds) heard at The Meadows, a small hiking path located in Highland Park, New Jersey. Visitors to the site can press the different clusters of letters to trigger on/off each grouping of sounds.

Lyrebird translates environmental sounds into vocal recreations, reflecting the human impulse to mimic our surroundings while revealing what emerges when our efforts encounter the limits of representation.
